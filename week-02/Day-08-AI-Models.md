# Week 2 - Day 8: AI Models 

## What is an AI Model?

An AI model is a trained system that learns patterns from data and performs tasks such as answering questions, generating text, writing code, or analyzing information.

## Popular AI Models

### 1. GPT
- Developed by OpenAI.
- Used in ChatGPT.
- Can generate text, write code, summarize documents, and answer questions.

# 2. Gemini
- Developed by Google.
- Supports text, images, audio, and video.
- Used for search, coding, and content generation.

# 3. Claude
- Developed by Anthropic.
- Known for long-document understanding and safe AI responses.

# 4. Llama
- Developed by Meta.
- Open-weight AI model that developers can use to build AI applications.

# 5. Mistral
- Developed by Mistral AI.
- Lightweight and efficient model used in many AI applications.

## Real-Life Example

When you ask ChatGPT to explain Python, GPT generates the answer.

When you ask Gemini to summarize a PDF, Gemini processes the document and generates a summary.

## Key Takeaways

- AI models are trained using large amounts of data.
- Different companies develop different AI models.
- AI models can answer questions, generate code, summarize text, and solve problems.
